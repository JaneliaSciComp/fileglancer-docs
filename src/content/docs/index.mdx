## What is Fileglancer?

Fileglancer is a web application designed specifically for researchers at Janelia Research Campus to easily browse, share, and manage scientific imaging data. It connects directly to Janelia's internal filesystems (PRFS/NRS/Nearline) without requiring data movement, making it simple to work with large imaging datasets.

If you encounter issues or need assistance, Fileglancer integrates with Janelia's help desk system for manual data conversions and technical support. Visit the [Troubleshooting](/support/troubleshooting/) section for common solutions or [Getting Help](/support/getting-help/) for support options.
